Alan Turing

1912 - 1954

Father of modern computing · Enigma codebreaker · AI visionary

The Genius of the 20th Century

Alan Mathison Turing (23 June 1912 - 7 June 1954) was a British mathematician, logician, cryptanalyst, and computer science pioneer. His work revolutionized:

  • Computing fundamentals
  • Modern cryptography
  • Artificial Intelligence
